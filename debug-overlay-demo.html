How It Works

It's Built Right Into WordPress

No external dashboards. No extra accounts. The overlay lives directly in your WordPress admin sidebar — always one click away while you browse your own site.

Activate the Plugin

Install DataLayer Tracker, go to Settings, and enable the debug overlay. It appears as a panel on your live site — visible only to admins.

Browse Normally

Visit your pages, add to cart, fill out forms. Every interaction fires automatically and appears in the overlay in real time.

Verify Your Tracking

Pin events, expand JSON payloads, and confirm that every event fires correctly before your data reaches GA4 or Meta.
